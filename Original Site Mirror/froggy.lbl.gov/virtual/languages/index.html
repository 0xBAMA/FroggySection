Dissection Kit Translations

The dissection kit is available in Español (Spanish), Deutsch (German), Nederlands (Dutch), Français (French), Italiano, and Portugues, thanks to the help of volunteer translators. (NOTE: some links from translated pages are to English pages.) These languages are also available through the setting of checkboxes in the dissection kit itself.

The Google translation service and Alta Vista's Babel Fish Translation were used for some individual words in the latest version of the kit. Please let me know if anything is incorrect.

Language statistics are available via a form button on the main information page.

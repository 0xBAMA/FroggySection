Honors for the Virtual Frog Dissection Kit

The dissection kit received a StudyWeb award in October of 1999.

It is listed on the BBC Education Web Guide.

It is listed in Educating.net.

It received a BioMedLink award in July of 1998.

It kit was chosen one of the 10 top Web sites by the editors of PC Computing in their December '95 issue.

It was chosen one of the top 100 Web sites by the editors of PC Magazine.

Fluffy, as part of the Whole Frog Project, was rated in the top 5% of all Web sites by Point Survey.

The dissection kit was also featured in the "Frog Stuff" category on Jeopardy in a high school tournament show in November of 1999.
